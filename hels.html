About Ryan Helsley

Ryan Helsley is a professional baseball player who currently pitches for the St. Louis Cardinals. He made his major league debut in 2019 and was a member of the Cardinals' bullpen during their 2020 postseason run. In his free time, he enjoys spending time with his dog, a Black Lab named Major.
