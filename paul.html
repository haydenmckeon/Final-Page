About Paul Goldschmidt

Paul Goldschmidt is a professional baseball player who currently plays first base for the St. Louis Cardinals. He is a six-time All-Star and has won three Gold Glove Awards and four Silver Slugger Awards. In his free time, he enjoys spending time with his dog, a Labrador Retriever named Maverick.
