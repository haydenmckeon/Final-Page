History

The Boston Red Sox are a professional baseball team based in Boston, Massachusetts. They were founded in 1901 and have won 9 World Series championships, the most recent in 2018.

Some of the most notable players in Red Sox history include Ted Williams, Carl Yastrzemski, David Ortiz, and Pedro Martinez.

Roster

  • Rafael Devers, third baseman
  • Chris Sale, starting pitcher
  • Matt Barnes, relief pitcher
