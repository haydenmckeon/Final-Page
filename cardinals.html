History

The St. Louis Cardinals are a professional baseball team based in St. Louis, Missouri. They were founded in 1882 and have won 11 World Series championships, the most recent in 2011.

Some of the most notable players in Cardinals history include Stan Musial, Bob Gibson, Lou Brock, and Ozzie Smith.

Roster

Some of the key players on the 2023 St. Louis Cardinals roster include:

  • Paul Goldschmidt, first baseman
  • Nolan Arenado, third baseman
  • Jack Flaherty, starting pitcher
  • Alex Reyes, relief pitcher
