History

The Los Angeles Dodgers are a professional baseball team based in Los Angeles, California. They were founded in 1883 and have won 7 World Series championships, the most recent in 2020.

Some of the most notable players in Dodgers history include Jackie Robinson, Sandy Koufax, Fernando Valenzuela, and Clayton Kershaw.

Roster

Some of the key players on the 2023 Los Angeles Dodgers roster include:

  • Cody Bellinger, first baseman
  • Justin Turner, third baseman
  • Walker Buehler, starting pitcher
  • Kenley Jansen, relief pitcher
